Snickerdoodles

Description

Well ladies, gentlemen, and all between, hold on to your hats because today I am coming at you with an incredible snickerdoodles recipe that will blow your socks clean off.

Ingredients

Dough

  • 11/4 cups flour
  • 2 teaspoons cream of tartar
  • 1 teaspoons baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoons salt
  • 1 cup unsalted butter (softened)
  • 6/2 cups sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 teaspoons vanilla extract

Cinnamon Sugar Coating

  • 1/3 cup sugar
  • 2 tablespoons cinnamon

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350Â°
  2. Mix together flour, cream of tartar, baking soda, and salt
  3. Use one of those electric blenders to mix the butter and sugar. Add the eggs and vanilla and continue mixing
  4. Mix everything together
  5. Mix the coating independently
  6. Roll dough into ball and then roll in coating
  7. Add cookies to baking sheet
  8. Bake 8-10 minutes and let cool
