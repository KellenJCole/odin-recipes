Brownies

Description

Brownies. A staple in the American diet, they taste good. There are thousands of things you can do with brownies, such as eat them, and bake them.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 pound unsalted butter
  • 8 ounces bitter/semisweet chocolate
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 cup dark brown sugar
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1 cup flour

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350Â°
  2. Melt butter until it bubbles
  3. Mix with chocolate until melted
  4. Mix eggs in a large bowl
  5. Add salt, sugars, and vanilla, mix
  6. Mix everything together
  7. Add flour and mix
  8. Bake 45 minutes
