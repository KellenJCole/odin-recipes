Pumpkin Pie

image source

Description

An absolutely scrumptious pumpkin pie that will certainly leave you craving more.

Takes one hour to make. Feeds six.

Ingredients

  • 16 ounces pumpkin puree
  • 3 large eggs
  • 2/3 cup sugar
  • 1 teaspoon cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon nutmeg
  • 1/2 teaspoon ginger
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 2/3 cup evaporated milk
  • Pie crust from the store ðŸ˜Ž

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees Fahrenheit
  2. In a large bowl, mix pumpkin puree, eggs, and sugar
  3. Mix in cinnamon, nutmeg, and ginger
  4. Slowly pour milks into mixture whilst mixing
  5. Pour into pie crust.
  6. If oven has reached 375Â°, place pie in oven
  7. Bake for 25 minutes or until it tastes incredible
